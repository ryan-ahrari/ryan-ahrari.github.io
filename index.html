Interpretability and Explainability Analysis Natural Language Processing (NLP) Project

This project analyzes several papers that overview the topics of explainability and interpretability in AI. There are three major sections of the project beginning with the literary analysis, following is the sentiment analysis, and finally the discussion. The literary analysis expounds on the important points of the papers and discusses several key aspects of the topics such as how they interact with other AI topics, and what it means to prioritize explainability and interpretability over optimality and bias. In the sentiment analysis with Flair, an NLP tool, an overwhelmingly positive sentiment in the way these two topics are being referred to is revealed. The project concludes by discussing that while the overwhelming positivity is beneficial, it may be difficult to get the monetary investment needed to create the explanations that will be necessary in the future.
